CRUSADE MINISTRY

With a vision and mission “Love God and Love people,” Dr.B.V.Kumar Foundation proclaim the Good News of Jesus Christ by every effective means to every nation, tribe and tongue to lead them into salvation and through salvation into eternal life. Dr.B.V.Kumar Foundation has been organising & conducting open air crusades from the past decades that includes Gospel Meetings, Revival Meetings, Healing Crusades, Deliverance Crusades, Youth Meetings, Women’s Conferences, Men’s Conferences etc at various remote interior villages, towns & cities throughout India and other nations. Multitudes have attended these meetings and have received the Lord as their personal saviour, lives changed & blessed, multitudes healed & delivered from sicknesses and bondages through the Life Changing messages and prayers by Dr.B.V.Kumar.
